Welcome To VehiSpot



YOUR JOURNEY, OUR COMMITMENT |||LET'S TRACK YOUR BUS |||

NBKR Institute of Science & Technology,we are dedicated to enhancing the commuting experience for our students and faculty members.Our Vehispot-Realtime vehicle tracking system is a testamant to our commitment to innovation and efficient in the world of transportation.A GPS-based vehicle tracking system is used to track vehicles from remote areas by using a website or application. Nowadays, faculty and students are missing their buses as their location is not identified. This Internet of Things (IoT) based vehicle tracking system helps to beat this issue. This product presents the implementation of an IOT-based system that permits folks, students, and even employees to trace the position of a college bus.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear - to revolutionize how our college community travels. We aim to provide real-time tracking and monitoring of our college buses, ensuring that every journey is as smooth and reliable as possible. Safety, convenience, and sustainability are at the heart of our project.As our college grows, so does our dedication to smart and sustainable transportation solutions. Our mission extends to adapting and expanding our services to meet the evolving needs of our college community.


Why VehiSpot?

Real-Time Tracking:
Our cutting-edge IoT technology allows you to track the exact location of our buses in real time, so you never have to wait in uncertainty.The tracking method that makes use of GPS as well as logistics databases to determine the current location of a person, vehicle, or object at any moment in time is known as Real-time Tracking.
Safety First:
prioritize your safety above all else. Our system offers alerts and notifications to keep you informed about any changes or emergencies.
Eco-Friendly:
By reducing unnecessary waiting times and optimizing routes, we contribute to a greener campus environment.
